About this report

PriceLens runs the equation the market just ran — in reverse. Given a price, it solves for the assumptions that price requires, ranks them by sensitivity, and surfaces the evidence behind each. Reasoning made auditable, not generated.

Methodology
  • └Reverse-DCF · 4Q trailing
  • └Monte Carlo intervals (p25/p50/p75)
  • └MiroThinker · evidence scoring
  • └Boundary detection · multi-framework
