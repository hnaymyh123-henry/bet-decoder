About Bet Decoder

Bet Decoder X-rays any investment bet — a market price, an analyst target, an opinion, or your whole portfolio. It decodes what each bet implicitly believes, stacks them side-by-side, and synthesizes cross-bet relations. Reasoning made auditable, not generated.

Methodology
  • └Reverse-DCF · 4Q trailing
